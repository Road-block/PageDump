RoadBlock - Today at 9:21 AM

Hiding some minimap buttons. - /st (this is for SpamThrottle) scroll down and uncheck [Display Minimap Button]
- /atlas > Click the Options button and select to show the minimap button and use the sliders below to position it
- /atlasloot > Click the Options button and select to hide it, check [EquipCompare] and Hide the Minimap Button (you can always access Atlasloot from Atlas, no need to have 2 buttons on minimap)
- DPSMate . Click Configure on the DPSMate window or right-click its button and Pick Configure from the menu. Go into [General Options] and uncheck [Show Minimap Button]
- /ctcp CTMod Control Panel. Click the [Movable Control Panel], Use the slider to move the CT icon around the Minimap to a free spot, Click the [Unlock Frames] button until your chat says "<CTMod> Frames have been locked"
Go to the Player Mods tab (2nd from bottom) and enable the [Casting Timer]
Finally go in the Misc. Mods tab (bottom one) and Set Chat Timestamp to "24h", Chat Scrolling to "2" and if you like [Relocate Tooltip] from bottom right to the top of the screen (up to you)
- /raoptions This will show the CT_RaidAssist options. Select the 3rd bullet [Misc Options] and then use the slider at the top to move the RA button to a free spot
That's the minimap situation sorted. Drag the remaining buttons around to arrange them so everything's visible.
Finally to place some onscreen elements of addons and you're done.
- Questie. Track a quest on the Questlog so the quest tracker is visible. Now Ctrl+Shift+Drag the tracker with the left-mouse button where you want it and release. Click one of the quest texts to show the OnScreen Arrow. Ctrl+Shift+Drag that with the left-mouse button too, where you want it (top center is a good spot imo)
- /cryo This is for Cryolysis. It's a great mage convenience addon. There's a lot of stuff to configure but you can play around. For now we only care for 2 things. 1. Go Timer Settings (2nd tab from bottom) and uncheck [Turn on the spell durations] and [Spelltimer Button]

RoadBlock - Today at 10:01 AM

2. Go Graphical Settings (bottom tab) and after dragging the Cryolysis sphere where you want it, select [Lock the buttons] and [Lock the Sphere]
Place KLHThreatMeter where you want it then use the [Pin] button to lock it.
Same for DPSMate, place it, size it from the bottom right corner then use the Configure menu to [Lock] it.
Finally Chronometer (your spell durations addon you had) and CooldownTimers (new addon to track your cooldowns) and we're done.
1. Chronometer /chroncl main anchor to show the anchor for moving the bars (use same command when placed where you want them to hide it) /chroncl bar width 175 /chroncl bar height 15 /chroncl bar test to see how it will look. All the options can also be accessed from the minimap button for Chronometer but this copy-pasting is simpler to do initial config. /chroncl main anchor again to hide the anchor.(edited)
2. CooldownTimers /cdtcl anchor to show the anchors for moving the bars and the text alerts when your cooldowns are up (use same comand when done to hide it) /cdtcl bar width 175 /cdtcl bar height 15 /cdtcl test to see how the bars will look. Same as before all options (and more) also available from the minimap button. /cdtcl anchor again to hide the anchor.(edited)
